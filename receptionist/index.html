Common questions

Will it actually sound like me?
Yes. During install we record your greeting, learn your services and pricing, and tune the agent's tone to match how you talk to customers. Most callers don't realize it's AI until we tell them.
Do I have to change my phone number?
No. We forward calls from your existing number when you can't pick up. Your business number stays the same; your customers don't notice anything different except that the line gets answered.
What if a call needs a real person?
The AI escalates immediately. If a customer says "I need to talk to someone" or describes a true emergency, the AI texts you with the caller's number and the situation, and offers to call them right back. You're always in the loop.
Does it work with my scheduling software?
We integrate with Google Calendar, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro, Jobber, and Mindbody out of the box. If you're using something else, we'll integrate it during install at no extra charge if it has an API.
What about overages? What if I get more than 500 conversations a month?
Most service businesses don't hit the cap. If you do, additional conversations are $0.50 each — billed monthly, no surprise charges. We'll alert you if you're trending toward the limit so there's no mystery.
How long is the contract?
There isn't one. Month-to-month after the initial 30 days. Cancel anytime. The setup fee covers the install work; if you cancel after that, we don't claw it back (but the 30-day guarantee covers your full risk).
How fast can you install it?
From signed contract to live agent: typically 3 business days. The install itself is under two hours of your time, mostly to record your greeting and walk us through your services and pricing.
